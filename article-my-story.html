From Accounting to Algorithms: My Journey to Software Engineering

January 2026

I’ve always wanted to be a builder — someone who turns ideas into products that didn’t exist before.

After graduating with a background in accounting and finance, I launched my first Amazon storefront from scratch. That experience taught me how execution, data, and design come together to drive business growth and customer experience.

Along the way, I became deeply fascinated by software — its scalability, creativity, and leverage in amplifying human potential. In China, I participated in and won two hackathons — one focused on an education tool, and another on a mental-health app — which strengthened my conviction to build technology that helps people learn and grow.

With the rise of AI lowering the barrier between imagination and creation, I decided to restart my journey as a computer science engineer, combining my foundation in business insight, data analytics, and product strategy to build intelligent, human-centered systems that empower focus, growth, and well-being.

Outside of work, I’m an active U.S. stock investor who enjoys analyzing companies, exploring market trends, and connecting with others who share a passion for tech and investing — feel free to connect!
